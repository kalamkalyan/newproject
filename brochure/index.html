About Therallen
Our Story

Empowering Partners Through Innovation

Therallen empowers customers to stay ahead of competition through divergent capabilities and differentiated products. We combine scientific depth with commercial intelligence to deliver formulations that create sustainable advantage in high-barrier therapeutic segments.

Leveraging global sales data, patent landscapes, and therapeutic segment insights, our R&D and innovation center iSentre (Hyderabad) and CGMP manufacturing facility in Nalagarh (Himachal Pradesh) enable deeper engagement across the customer growth chain — from development to commercial supply.

Value Proposition

A one-stop partner for complex oral solid dosage development — pelletisation, MUPS, combination products, and direct compressible granules — backed by IP-guided, non-infringing formulation pathways and regulatory-ready dossiers.

Who We Serve

  • Regional partners in Latin America, Middle East & CIS markets
  • Organizations requiring tech-transfer and licensing opportunities
  • Customers needing nitrosamine-safe, quality-assured formulations
